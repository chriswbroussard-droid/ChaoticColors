Fluid Frontier Series

Fluid Frontiers explores the chaotic elegance of liquid dynamics through the lens of abstract expressionism. This series captures the tension between opposing elemental forces—the searing heat of volcanic flows and the crushing depths of the oceanic abyss. By utilizing fluid acrylic techniques, these works emphasize the organic interplay of pigment and gravity. Each piece serves as a topographical map of a world in constant flux, where color acts as a conduit for movement. From the subterranean glows of Abyssal Dawn to the energetic rise of Verdant Tides Ascending, the collection invites the viewer to find stillness within the rush of the current.

Luminous Liquidity

Luminous Liquidity explores the tension between deliberate composition and the chaotic beauty of fluid dynamics. This series captures a frozen state of motion, where vibrant pigments collide and interact to form complex, organic structures. Azure Ember and Magenta Monsoon highlight the volatile meeting of warm and cool tones, mimicking natural phenomena of heat and pressure. Bismuth Bloom and Indigo Marrow delve into cellular textures, suggesting the internal, microscopic architectures of life and minerals. Through the medium of acrylic pouring, these works emphasize the luminosity of color suspended in a state of flow. The series serves as a visual meditation on the unpredictability of the physical world, inviting the viewer to find order within the sprawl of liquid abstraction.

Cellular Synthesis Series

Cellular Synthesis is an exploration of the microscopic patterns that govern the macroscopic world. By utilizing fluid dynamics and chemical reactions between pigments, this series bridges the gap between biological structures and celestial phenomena. The collection focuses on the emergence of "cells"—organic, self-organizing shapes that manifest through the tension of the medium. From the deep, pressurized hues of Amethyst Abyss to the rhythmic, heat-mapped topography of Terracotta Tides, the work examines how nature repeats its most efficient designs across vastly different scales. Whether mimicking the protective plating in Leviathan's Armor or the chaotic flow of Crimson Current, these pieces serve as a visual meditation on the invisible forces that bind the natural universe together.

Chromatic Flux Series

Chromatic Flux explores the intersection of organic geometry and fluid dynamics. Through a vivid spectrum of high-contrast pigments, the series captures a sense of perpetual motion suspended in time. Each piece serves as a topographical study of heat and energy, ranging from the rhythmic, concentric undulations of solar radiation to the chaotic, cellular interactions of molten earth. By juxtaposing structured patterns with unpredictable pours, the collection invites the viewer to observe the raw, elemental forces that shape both the microscopic world and the vast cosmos.

Batanical Burst Series

Botanical Burst reimagines traditional floral subjects through the lens of abstract expressionism and fluid art. This series emphasizes the explosive energy inherent in nature, where petals are no longer static objects but dynamic events of color and movement. By employing diverse techniques—from the crisp, icy textures of frost to the fiery, cellular reactions of a solar flare—the collection captures the fleeting vitality of life. Each piece invites the viewer to look beyond the surface of a flower and experience the raw, pulsing force of growth and decay.

About the Artist

Art has been my shadow since I could walk, but it took a detour through 35 years of being a haircolor specialist to find its true form. Based in Austin, TX, I'm an experimentalist at heart who swapped hair dye for high-flow acrylics.

I use my deep-rooted knowledge of color theory to orchestrate collisions between chemistry and creativity. The result? Macro-organic patterns that celebrate the beauty of the unpredictable. I don't paint static images; I capture movement suspended in time.

Working with professional-grade acrylics and innovative pouring techniques, Chris creates vibrant, one-of-a-kind artworks that capture movement and emotion in abstract form. Each series represents a unique exploration of theme, palette, and methodology.

Original works will be secured and sold to collectors upon request. Because of the volatile nature of the fluid process, these specific movements, color bleeds, and textures can never be replicated.
